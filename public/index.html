About Mindcomplete

An AI-powered writing tool that completes your thoughts as you type. Pause for a moment, and the AI will suggest how to continue your paragraph, matching your tone and style.

How It Works

  1. Start typing your thoughts
  2. Pause for 300ms - AI starts thinking
  3. "Continuing..." appears while waiting
  4. AI suggestion appears in dimmed text
  5. Hover over the suggestion - text turns white up to your cursor
  6. Click anywhere or press TAB to accept up to that point
  7. Keep writing naturally!
