How It Works

  1. Start typing your thoughts
  2. Pause for 1 second - AI starts thinking
  3. "typing..." appears while waiting
  4. AI suggestion appears in dimmed text
  5. Hover over the suggestion - text turns white up to your cursor
  6. Click anywhere to accept up to that point
  7. Drag to select a portion in the middle - only that part gets accepted, everything before is skipped, everything after remains
  8. Press TAB to accept the full suggestion
  9. Keep writing naturally!
