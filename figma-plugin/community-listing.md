# Mindcomplete: AI Writing Partner

**AI that writes WITH you, not for you.**

That was the main idea behind this tool. You just start writing, and Mindcomplete continues for you when you stop.

Instead of acting based on prompts, it analyzes what you have written, along with selected text and images, to understand exactly what you're working on.

Powered with Google's Gemini, it uses the same smart AI, but at this time, it's not on top of your mind, but it's a natural extension to your thoughts.
